Our Mission

We're turning Pittsburgh into a comedy powerhouse! Our festival is packed with top-notch comedy short films from our own yinzers and funny folks from all over the globe. Expect more laughs than you can shake a pierogi at! Here's what's comin' dahn the road:

  • Hilarious Screenings: A curated selection of the funniest films from around the world and from our talented local filmmakers.
  • Live Comedy Acts: Film blocks hosted by Pittsburgh's top standup comics.
  • Insightful Q&As: Interactive sessions with filmmakers.
  • Networking Opportunities: Connect with industry professionals, fellow filmmakers, and comedy enthusiasts.
  • Cookies: Enjoy a Pittsburgh-style cookie table at the award ceremony.

Film Submission

Submissions Closed

The submission deadline for the Cookies and Comedy Film Festival has passed. Thank you to all who submitted their films!

Filmmakers around the world, submit your comedy films to the Cookies and Comedy Film Festival!

Submissions Closed

Submission Guidelines:

  • Genre: Films must be primarily comedy.
  • Completion Date: Films should have been completed after January 1, 2023.
  • Language: Non-English films must include English subtitles.
  • Length: Films should not exceed 20 minutes.

Submission Requirements:

  • Film Synopsis (50 words max)
  • Country of Origin
  • Languages Spoken
  • Genres (if any in addition to comedy)
  • Length of Film
  • Prior Festival Screenings
  • Honors and Awards
  • Filmmaker Contact Information
  • Director's Bio (100 words max, optional)
  • Personal Statement (100 words max, optional)

Key Dates:

  • Submission Deadline: December 31, 2025
  • Notification Date: January 14, 2026
  • Festival Date: March 14, 2026
