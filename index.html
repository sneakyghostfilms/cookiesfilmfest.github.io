Our Venue

Harris Theater

Our festival takes place at the Harris Theater in downtown Pittsburgh's Cultural District, a beloved art-house cinema operated by the Pittsburgh Cultural Trust.

The historic marquee and Liberty Avenue entrance give the day a real sense of occasion before audiences head inside for films, Q&As, live comedy, and cookies.

Film Submission

Submissions Open June 1

Submissions for the 2027 festival open Monday, June 1, 2026. The festival returns to the Harris Theater on Saturday, March 13, 2027.

Submission Guidelines:

  • Genre: Films must be primarily comedy.
  • Completion Date: Films should have been completed after January 1, 2026.
  • Language: Non-English films must include English subtitles.
  • Length: Films should not exceed 20 minutes.
  • Awards: Selected films will be considered for festival awards. Award categories will be announced closer to the event.

AI & Synthetic Media Policy (2027)

The Cookies & Comedy Film Festival recognizes that AI tools are increasingly part of modern filmmaking workflows. Rather than attempting to regulate every possible use, the festival is making a focused, pragmatic choice about character and performance.

The following are not permitted:

  • AI-generated characters, including foreground or background characters. This includes AI-generated faces, bodies, voices, or performances intended to function as characters in the film.
  • Synthetic replicas of real people (voice or likeness) without explicit consent from the individual depicted (and legal guardians where applicable).

This policy prioritizes human performance and authorship, and is intentionally aligned with evolving labor protections for performers and creators.

As labor organizations such as SAG-AFTRA establish clearer standards, consent models, and compensation frameworks for AI-generated character usage, the festival will revisit this policy accordingly.
