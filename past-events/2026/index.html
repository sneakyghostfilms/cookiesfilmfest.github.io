2026 Festival Recap

Back to Main Site

Our 2nd annual festival in 2026 was an incredible day of laughs! Held at the Harris Theater in Pittsburgh's Cultural District, we showcased over 40 comedy films from around the world across three themed blocks, each hosted by talented local standup comedians. Festival Director Scott Danzig once again baked hundreds of homemade cookies, facilitated Q&A sessions with attending filmmakers, and recorded audience reactions for each film as special mementos sent to all creators.
